Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

FuelGenius is provided by FuelGenius Dev in Bulgaria. Contact: fuelgenius_support@napomniapp.site.

What the current web build stores

  • Fuel history entered by the user
  • Language and theme preferences
  • Offline cache files required for the PWA
  • Privacy choices related to optional analytics and advertising

These items are stored locally on the device through browser storage. The current static web build does not send user-entered fuel records to FuelGenius Dev servers.

Optional analytics and advertising

The files of this project are prepared for future integration with analytics and advertising providers such as Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob. These optional services are disabled by default until all of the following are true:

  1. a valid provider ID has been added in release-config.js or the Android wrapper,
  2. the final app build actually contains the relevant SDK or service, and
  3. the user has granted consent where consent is legally required.

If enabled in a future release, those providers may process device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, and advertising data under their own policies. You must update this policy again before publishing such a release.

Legal basis for optional processing

For users in the EEA, UK, or other regions with similar requirements, analytics and personalized advertising should be activated only after valid user consent. Strictly necessary storage for the app itself remains active because it is needed for core functionality.

Sharing

The current web build does not intentionally share user-entered calculation history with third parties. If analytics, ads, crash reporting, or other third-party SDKs are later enabled in the Android app or wrapper, data may be processed by those providers according to the final released configuration.

User controls

  • You can delete saved records from the History screen.
  • You can clear all saved records from the app at any time.
  • You can change privacy choices from the app footer.
  • You can clear local storage and cached files from your browser or device settings.

Children

The app is not directed to children under the minimum age required by local law. If you later add ads or analytics, review Google Play Families and age-rating requirements before release.
