Cookies & Storage Notice

Last updated: 30 March 2026

FuelGenius uses local device storage for core functionality and is prepared for optional analytics/advertising consent management.

Strictly necessary storage

  • localStorage for saved fuel history, language, theme, and privacy choices
  • Service Worker Cache for offline files such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and icons

This storage is necessary for the app to function correctly and does not require consent where local law exempts strictly necessary storage.

Optional technologies

The project is prepared for future analytics and advertising integrations. Optional processing must stay disabled until the final release is configured and the user has granted consent where required.

How to manage choices

Users can open Privacy Choices from the app footer to review or update optional settings. Clearing browser or app storage will also remove the saved consent state.

How to clear data

Users can delete saved entries from the History screen, clear all records inside the app, or remove browser/app storage from device settings.
